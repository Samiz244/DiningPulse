# Product Spec — Partner Signup Landing Page

## Problem
DiningPulse currently lacks a clear landing page for restaurant owners or managers who may want to become partners. Without a dedicated partner-facing page, potential customers do not have a clear way to understand the product, its value, or how to express interest.

## Users
- restaurant owners
- restaurant managers
- operators evaluating restaurant technology

## Outcome
Create a landing page that explains DiningPulse clearly and gives interested restaurants a way to submit their information for follow-up.

## Scope
In scope:
- partner-facing landing page
- clear value proposition
- product overview sections
- call-to-action
- partner interest form

Out of scope:
- full onboarding portal
- billing
- contract signing
- dashboard creation
- live partner provisioning

## Non-Goals
- building the full partner management system
- automating approval workflows
- integrating payments
- creating authenticated partner accounts

## Acceptance Criteria
- user can understand what DiningPulse does within a few seconds
- page clearly explains benefits for restaurant operators
- page includes a clear call-to-action
- user can submit interest form successfully
- form handles failure state visibly
- page works on desktop and mobile
- no fake success state is shown

## Risk Level
MEDIUM
